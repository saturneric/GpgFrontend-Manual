## Who Is It For?

GpgFrontend fits whatever you need to protect:

- **Anyone who values privacy**: keep your personal files, photos, and messages
  for your eyes only.
- **Journalists and activists**: protect sources and sensitive information.
- **Developers**: sign your software releases so people know they are genuine.
- **Teams and businesses**: keep separate keys for work, clients, or projects.

Whether you are brand new to encryption or an everyday expert, GpgFrontend adapts
to how you work.

## New Here? Start in 3 Steps

Never used encryption before? You can be up and running in a few minutes:

1. [Install GpgFrontend](/overview/getting-started/) for your system.
2. [Make your first key](/guides/generate-key/), then back it up somewhere safe.
3. [Encrypt a file](/guides/file-operations/) or
   [some text](/guides/text-operations/).

Not sure what a "key" even is? Start with
[Fundamental Concepts](/guides/fundamental-concepts/). It explains everything in
plain words, no background needed.
